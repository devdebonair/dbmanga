10,000+ Manga Titles

We have an extensive database filled with over 10,000 different manga titles. These titles are updated regularly and will all be available for free. All it takes is a simple request by you to add a title.

Video Reviews

We are currently working on a system to integrate video reviews into the manga experience. Series and chapter reviews will be aggretated for your benefit. Share your experience and watch how others react to the chapter you just read.

Interactive

Interactive comments will be available for each page you read. One word reactions from different users will keep your experience social but discrete. Check out everyone's reaction to that draw dropping moment in your favorite manga title.

Responsive Design

Although plans for iOS, Windows, and Android apps are on our roadmap, our site will be fully responsive and accessible to mobile devices. You will have the same experience on and off the browser. Perfect for on the go reading.

Stay in Sync

Once released, your account will be in sync with all of your devices. From the browser, save manga titles to your library and have them available for offline viewing on your phone or tablet.

Our Mission

Responsive and Interactive Manga Experience

Anime has become very influential in the West because of hit animes like Naruto, One Piece, and Attack on Titan. With that attraction, came manga, the originator of most animes. Most manga do not get translated to english, leaving a void for scanlation aggregators like MangaPanda to take advantage of. This causes a huge problem because this is considered piracy of copyrighted material. There is a legal gray area because most manga do not make it to the West which do not affect sales in Japan. The problem is that aggregators lack presentation and feature. Websites are layed out to take advantage of ad revenue as opposed to make it easier and convenient for the consumer. We want to fill that void. Not only that, but we want to push scanlations out of that legal gray area. Crunchyroll, Viz Media, and other content providers have attempted to solve the issue but have either relied on old tactics to sell volumes, or have simply denied focus on manga. We are here to provide the features that consumers want. We are here to focus on manga. We are here to make scanlations legal and boost the sales of related content providers. Debonair Manga has a creative team, a streamlined focus, young ambition, and a big roadmap. Help us help you.
